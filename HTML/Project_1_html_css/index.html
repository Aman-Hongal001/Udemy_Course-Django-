Startup India Standup India is a scheme launched by the Modi government on 16th of January in 2016. This campaign is planned to bring new opportunities for the youths of the country. PM Narendra Modi was talked about this campaign on 15th of August while addressing the nation from Red Fort, New Delhi.

This initiative is to encourage the young entrepreneurs to greatly involve in the entrepreneurship for better future. According to the programme, around 1.25 lakh bank branches encourage youth (at least one Dalit or Adivasi and one woman entrepreneur) entrepreneurs by giving them loan. This campaign creates new jobs for the people in India.
